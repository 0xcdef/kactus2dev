Step 2: Accessing Documents

  1. Select VLNV tree.
  2. Browse to the desired document, double click to open.
  3. Right click for context menu. The additional options include opening and creating designs.
  4. The external interfaces and ad-hoc ports are illustrated in preview.
  5. You need to open protection (ctrl + L) to modify the document. It is protected again with the same button or hotkey, or when protection of another document is opened.
